Graduate Student Housing Characteristics

Early during my graduate studies, I recognized the difficulty that graduate students in my program had in finding affordable housing in the greater Seattle area, and the utility of data for informed decision-making. Information about housing quality and costs is also invaluable to prospective students who are deciding whether to come to Seattle. In 2017 I started a housing survey for graduate students in my program, designed to collect data about neighborhoods, rents, and other pertinent details. For the first few years the survey was ad hoc as I refined questions and improved my data analysis; I also worked with UAW 4121, the academic employees union, to develop a version of the survey that was used during contract negotiations. In 2020 I developed a more streamlined, repeatable script to analyze the survey responses, which made it easier to share the analysis with current and prospective students.
