Research

I recently earned a PhD in glaciology under the supervision of Dr. Ian Joughin, in the Department of Earth and Space Sciences at the University of Washington. My research focused on using satellite images to map changes in outlet glacier extent on time scales ranging from weeks to decades. I used this information to study how these glaciers are responding to changes in their environment. I am also interested in ice-ocean interactions, glaciers as water resources, and how glaciers influence local ecology.

I focus specifically in these areas:

  • Multidecadal trends — climate drivers of long-term regional retreat
  • Seasonal variability — prevalance and timing of seasonal variations
