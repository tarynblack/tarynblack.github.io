Research

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Earth System Science Interdisciplinary Center at the University of Maryland and NASA GSFC. My research focuses on satellite remote sensing observations of changes to Earth's ice sheets and glaciers. I use measurements of ice extent and elevation change to study how glaciers are responding to changes in their environment, on seasonal to decadal time scales.
