Research

I am pursuing a PhD in glaciology under the supervision of Dr. Ian Joughin, in the Department of Earth and Space Sciences at the University of Washington. My primary focus is on using satellite images to map changes in outlet glacier extent on time scales ranging from weeks to decades. I use this information to study how these glacier are responding to changes in their environment. I am also interested in ice-ocean interactions, glaciers as water resources, and how glaciers influence local ecology.

I focus specifically in these areas:

  • Multidecadal trends — climate drivers of long-term regional retreat
  • Seasonal variability — prevalance and timing of seasonal variations and connection with glacial dynamics
